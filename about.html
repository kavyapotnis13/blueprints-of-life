About

From creating better vaccines, engineering novel prosthetics, and optimizing physiological processes for greater health, biotechnology is everywhere. However, with these rapid advancements, there exists an increasing knowledge gap between scientific development and the general public. Science is littered with jargon, locked behind paywalls, and generally is inaccessible to everyday consumers. This can result in misinterpretation, misinformation, and a general distrust of science or misuse of such advancements.

Blueprints of Life aims to close this gap by providing podcast episodes with scientists providing descriptions of their work and its impact in an accessible way for everyone.
